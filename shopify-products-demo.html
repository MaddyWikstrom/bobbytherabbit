Shopify Products with Real Variant IDs

This demo loads real products directly from your Shopify store with correct variant IDs

Quick Setup

  1. Open scripts/load-products.js
  2. Replace your-store.myshopify.com with your actual Shopify domain
  3. Replace your-storefront-access-token with your Storefront API token
  4. Refresh this page to see your real products

Note: You can get a Storefront API token from your Shopify Admin → Apps → "App and sales channel settings" → Develop apps and channels
